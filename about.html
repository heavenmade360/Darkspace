Origin story visual
How it started

A timer app born out of frustration.

In 2022, our founder Marcus was finishing his master's thesis and bouncing between five different apps — a timer here, a task list there, a habit tracker on his phone. None of them talked to each other. None of them understood how he actually worked.

He spent a weekend building a simple timer that tracked what he was working on. He shared it with friends. By Monday morning, 200 people were using it.

That weekend project became FocusFlow. Today, 150,000 people use it every day to do their best work.

Our mission is simple: make focused work the default.

Every feature we build, every design decision we make, every line of code we ship is evaluated against one question — does this help people do deeper, better work? If the answer isn't a clear yes, we don't ship it.
